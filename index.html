The quick answers

FAQ

Is TouchGrass really free?

Yep. Download builds from GitHub releases or run it from source. No ads, no tracking, just healthier work habits.

Will it work with my OS?

TouchGrass ships for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Built with Tauri, it feels native wherever you work.

Can I contribute?

Absolutely. Check the roadmap, open an issue, or submit a pull request. Friendly sarcasm included.

Does it need internet?

Nope. Reminders all happen locally, and your preferences stay on your machine.
